Mango & Passion Fruit Smoothie

Description

A tropical fruit blend to start the day the nutritious way - full of vitamin C and low in fat too.

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 400g peeled and chopped ripe mango
  • 2 x 125g pots fat-free mango yogurt
  • 250ml skimmed milk
  • 1 juiced lime
  • 4 passion fruits, halved

Instructions

  1. Whizz the mango, yogurt and milk together in a blender until smooth. Stir in the lime juice, then pour into 4 glasses. Scoop the pulp of a passion fruit into each one, and swirl before serving.
